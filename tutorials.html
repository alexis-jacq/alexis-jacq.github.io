Neural transfert of style with Pytorch

This tutorial explains how to use a pre-trained convolutional neural network in order to transfert the artistic style of a painting on a picture of your choice. What we call "style" here can be defined by the statistical properties of the latent hidden feature maps, used to detect and classify objects.
